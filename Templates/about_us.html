ABOUT US

This project was born out of a shared passion for food and a desire to create a space where students could gather, socialize, and enjoy delicious, affordable meals. Our team of 7 dedicated individuals came together with a vision to transform the traditional cafeteria experience into a vibrant and inviting dining destination.

Our Story

Once upon a time, in ICTU university, a group of eight friends dreamed of more than just textbooks and homework. They wanted to create something special, something that would bring joy and nourishment to their school community. So, they decided to open a restaurant! With their hearts full of passion and their hands ready to work, they transformed a small, empty space into a cozy and inviting dining area. They learned about cooking, food safety, and customer service, . Now, their restaurant is a place where students, teachers, and staff come together to enjoy delicious meals and make lasting memories. It's a testament to their hard work, dedication, and the power of friendship.
